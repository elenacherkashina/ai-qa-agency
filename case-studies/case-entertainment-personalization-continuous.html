Client Context

The client operates one of the largest event discovery platforms in Europe, offering users tailored suggestions for concerts, experiences, and activities based on intent, season, and location. Their AI assistant continuously evolves through new event data and retrained recommendation models.

Challenge

They needed recurring QA cycles to validate dynamic personalization logic, ensure high relevance of suggestions, and catch regressions caused by silent changes in data inputs or model behavior.

Our Work

  • Weekly prompt-based validation of user journeys (anonymous, logged-in, new vs returning)
  • Regression testing of top personalization patterns by intent and category
  • Edge-case scenario design for mood-based prompts, travel-based filters, and seasonal rules

Deliverables

  • Monthly QA reports with recommendation alignment scores
  • Annotated prompt response matrix
  • Automation assets: scalable test suite updated monthly

Outcome

Within 2 months, weekly misalignment in top recommendations dropped from 17% to 3%. Internal content teams used QA outputs to prioritize fixes in tone, flow logic, and tagging inconsistencies.
