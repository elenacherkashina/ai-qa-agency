AI Chatbot QA for E-commerce

Package: One-Time QA Audit ($3K)

Client Context

The client, a fast-growing online fashion brand, developed a shopping assistant to help users discover products, apply discounts, and ask delivery questions. The assistant was integrated across web and mobile, with early positive feedback from internal testing.

Challenge

Before going live, the client needed an independent QA audit to ensure the chatbot handled real-world edge cases, maintained brand tone across all flows, and personalized the experience for both new and returning users.

Our Work

  • Tested 30+ interaction flows across key customer journeys
  • Audited prompt clarity, fallback responses, and personalization logic
  • Evaluated tone consistency and session memory handling

Deliverables

  • Executive QA summary with annotated journey maps
  • List of 11 critical UX and logic inconsistencies
  • Prompt refinement pack and fallback improvements
  • Automation assets: executable test suite for critical chatbot flows

Outcome

After implementing our recommendations, the client saw a 21% increase in product clicks via the assistant and a 14% drop in conversation drop-offs. Escalations to human support decreased during peak hours.
